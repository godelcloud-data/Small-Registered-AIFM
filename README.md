**Description:** A Small Registered AIFM is one of the three regulatory classifications of UK alternative investment fund managers (AIFMs), with the other two being Full-Scope AIFM and Small Authorised AIFM. Of the 1,284 total UK AIFMs at at Q4 2025, Full-Scope AIFMs form the majority with 685 firms (53.3%), subject to the full obligations under the UK AIFM Regime. Small Authorised AIFMs account for 466 firms (36.3%), and Small Registered AIFMs comprise 133 firms (10.4%) under lighter registration with limited scope.
